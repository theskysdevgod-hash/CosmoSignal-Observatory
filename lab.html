üìö What is Comet 3I/ATLAS?

3I/ATLAS is an interstellar comet discovered in 2017. It's only the second interstellar comet ever observed by humans, making it an extraordinary subject for scientific study. This comet originated from outside our solar system and provides valuable insights into the composition and behavior of comets from other star systems.
